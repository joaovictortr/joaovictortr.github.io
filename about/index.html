About

  • Jan 4, 2020
  • One minute read

I am João Victor Risso. I am employed at Nubank in São Paulo as a data analyst. I earned a Masters in Computer Science (in High Performance Computing) from the Federal University of Paraná, Curitiba. I also did my undergraduate degree in Computer Science at the Federal University of Paraná, and I was lucky to go on two exchange programs with the Friedrich-Alexander University in Erlangen, where I've worked with the waLBerla framework.

My interests revolve around:

  1. Design, algorithms and operations of large-scale data-intensive systems.
  2. Privacy and anonymity online

This blog is a presentation of my learnings, ideas I have, solutions I come across, notes on certain topics. In short, my explorations for knowledge that are (hopefully) useful for readers.

All opinions emitted in this blog are my own and do not reflect the views of my current or past employers.
